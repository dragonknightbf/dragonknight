Stone Legion Generals

Hero in phase 3

The two generals swap places for phase 1 and 2 until coming together for phase 3

All bleed debuffs are permanent and can only be removed with the combo

Focus kill when they spawn

Intermissions

Intermission phase starts when the current general reaches 50%

The general will continue casting their abilities and spawning adds

During intermission, players must collect to charge Renethal up and get out of the intermission
